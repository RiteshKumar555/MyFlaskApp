IBSAT

s an important online computer based MBA entrance test conducted across India by ICFAI Foundation for Higher Education (a deemed to be University). IBSAT 2017 is the gateway to MBA/PGPM 2018-20 at 9 campuses of ICFAI Business School (IBS). The exam will be conducted in the month of December 2017 in a 4 day test window from December 21-24, 2017. It will be a 2 hour computer based test. Those who wish to pursue MBA/PGPM/PhD program from IBS B-schools have to appear for IBSAT2017 Test scheduled in the month of December 2017. IBSAT 2017 application form is available till December 12 at the link www.ibsindia.org/ibsat2017. Exam Format:

  • Testing Mode of IBSAT 2018: Online Computer Based Test
  • IBSAT 2018 Testing Window: Exam will be conducted in a testing window of 2 days on December 22 and 23, 2018.
  • IBSAT 2018 Test Duration: The exam will be of 2 hours duration
  • IBSAT 2018 Test Sections: The exam will be divided into 4 Sections
  • Total Questions: 140
  • Type of Questions: Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
  • Difficulty Level: Easy to Moderate
  • Sectional Time Limit: No Sectional Time Limit. You can switch from one section to other within overall time limit of 2 hours
  • Scoring Pattern: +1 Mark for each correct answer
  • Negative Marking: No Negative marking is there in IBSAT
